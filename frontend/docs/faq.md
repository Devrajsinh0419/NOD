# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Here are the most frequently asked questions about the Night Owl Designers (NOD) platform.

### How do I get started with a new design project?
To start, sign up or log in to the dashboard, complete your client profile, and select the desired service (e.g., Interior Design, 2D/3D Floor Plan) under the "Services" section. You can submit initial requirements and schedule a consultation directly.

### What is the typical timeline for a 2D/3D floor plan?
Standard 2D layout drafts are usually delivered within 3 to 5 business days. 3D renderings and walkthroughs take between 7 to 10 business days depending on design complexity.

### Does NOD support Vastu Shastra during planning?
Yes, we offer integrated Vastu consultation. If requested, our architects will design your floor plans and layouts in accordance with ancient Vastu Shastra principles to ensure natural light, wind, and spatial energies are optimized.

### Can I request a renovation for a single room?
Yes, our Renovation pillar supports both complete building overhauls and single-space modifications (e.g., kitchen remodeling, living room updates, office workspace renovations).

### How are construction costs estimated?
Estimates are calculated based on built-up area (per square foot), structural design details, and choices of materials (premium vs. standard). A detailed quotation is uploaded to your client dashboard after the structural planning phase.

### Are structural drawings checked for safety?
Absolutely. All structural plans, concrete schedules, and load-bearing designs are reviewed and stamped by certified structural engineers to ensure maximum safety, seismic resilience, and adherence to local building codes.
