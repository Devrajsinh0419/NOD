# Platform Features & Services

Night Owl Designers (NOD) offers three primary pillars of services, all managed through our state-of-the-art client dashboard.

## 1. Designs Pillar
Custom architectural and interior layouts crafted to blend elegance with utility.
* **Interior Design**: Tailored aesthetics, lighting layouts, and custom furniture placement for residential and commercial spaces.
* **Exterior Design**: Modern facades, materials sourcing lists, elevation planning, and landscape integration.
* **2D & 3D Floor Plans**: Immersive 3D visualization rendering and detailed 2D structural layout blueprints.

## 2. Construction Pillar
Precision-driven construction services built with quality materials, efficiency, and safety.
* **Civil Construction**: Comprehensive building construction from excavation and foundation to completion.
* **Renovation Services**: Remodeling, retrofitting, and updating older structures with modern materials and layouts.
* **Structural Planning**: Scientific load distribution, steel scheduling, and concrete reinforcement layouts.

## 3. Technical Planning Pillar
Technical drafting and consultation services matching industrial standards and cultural practices.
* **AutoCAD Drafting**: Precision computerized drafting for architectural, mechanical, and electrical layouts.
* **Vastu Consultation**: Architectural planning aligned with traditional Vastu Shastra principles for harmony and energy.
* **Project Planning**: Phase-by-phase project schedules, resource allocation charts, and cost estimation.

## 4. Digital Dashboard Features
Clients and professionals collaborate using our unified portal:
* **Interactive Client Profile**: Set preferences, view active projects, and update personal credentials.
* **Project Status Tracker**: Track design iterations, construction progress, and approval milestones in real-time.
* **Document Locker**: Safe cloud storage for blue prints, CAD files, estimation sheets, and contracts.
