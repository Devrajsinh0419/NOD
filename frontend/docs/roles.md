# Platform Roles & Responsibilities

The Night Owl Designers (NOD) platform uses role-based access control (RBAC) to separate duties and optimize workflows. Here are the active roles:

## 1. Client
* **Description**: Homeowners, commercial buyers, or project sponsors who seek design, planning, or construction services.
* **Responsibilities**:
  * Set up and maintain user profiles.
  * Submit design requirements and site measurements.
  * Review, comment on, and approve 2D/3D layouts.
  * Make payments and track construction progress.

## 2. Designer (Interior/Exterior)
* **Description**: Creative professionals responsible for space styling and structural aesthetics.
* **Responsibilities**:
  * Create mood boards, material lists, and layout options.
  * Prepare and upload high-fidelity 3D visualizations.
  * Review design iterations based on client feedback.

## 3. Architect
* **Description**: Technical designers focused on building layouts, elevations, and AutoCAD drafting.
* **Responsibilities**:
  * Produce precise 2D blueprints and floor plans.
  * Ensure structural aesthetics conform to local building regulations.
  * Provide Vastu consultation parameters to designers.

## 4. Project Manager (Construction Lead)
* **Description**: Site engineers and construction coordinators who execute structural plans in the physical world.
* **Responsibilities**:
  * Manage site personnel, materials, and machinery.
  * Update daily progress logs and pictures on the client dashboard.
  * Verify safety protocols and execute structural plan deadlines.

## 5. Administrator (Admin)
* **Description**: Platform operators who manage global users, approve portfolios, and handle financial operations.
* **Responsibilities**:
  * Moderate community listings and designer profiles.
  * Troubleshoot client billing and project matching.
  * Audit project safety logs and system integrations.
